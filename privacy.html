Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We differentiate personal and non-personal data. Personal data is information that can be used to uniquely identify a specific individual.

We collect anonymized data to determine overall usage statistics of our website and our apps. In apps, we use Firebase Analytics SDKs to understand feature usage and device characteristics.

Crash Reports

When a crash occurs in our apps, we automatically collect anonymized crash reports using Firebase Crashlytics to help debug issues.

Cookie Policy

Accounts and associated data can be deleted within the app or by submitting a request via our contact page.
